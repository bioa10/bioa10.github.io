Chessmaster


Battle is no different than a game of Chess. Like chess, my first move has already decided your fate.

The Chessmaster tactically leads his allies to victory through unparalleled teamwork and camaraderie. Always able to imagine not only the battlefield, but the mindsets of enemy and ally alike, the Chessmaster is able to guide the flow of battle in whatever way he sees fit.
