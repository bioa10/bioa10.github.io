The Red Swamp

by bioa10



As new "heroes" you have been trying to find your first job when you recieve a letter from a man named Lord Redspar. He has provided very few details, though you know that if you complete a job for him you will quickly rise in renown. You've heard rumors of some trouble in the Red Swamp and wonder if this is about that. What kinds of foul beasts will you have to deal with?


This is a short GMless adventure intended to be run with 3-4 players and take 3-4 hours. This is mostly a test adventure for me to prepare for the real adventure I would like to make. Have fun!
